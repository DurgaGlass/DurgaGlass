About

For over 25 years, Durga Laboratory has been a trusted name in the production and export of high-quality Laboratory Glassware and Instruments, proudly serving customers in 15+ countries worldwide. Operating from a single, fully integrated facility with a workforce of over 600 skilled employees, we produce a wide range of standard glassware that adheres to ISO and BIS standards.

In line with our commitment to innovation and growth, we have recently expanded our capabilities to include the manufacturing of Disposable Glassware, such as Test Tubes, Pipettes, and Culture Tubes. This development marks a significant step in our journey, enabling us to serve a broader range of laboratory needs.

Each product listed in this catalogue comes with a unique Product Code, which can be scanned to instantly access our website for detailed product information and a quick-buy option, offering you a seamless purchasing experience.

At Durga Laboratory, we believe in building strong, lasting relationships with our clients. Understanding your specific needs allows us to deliver the best solutions in the shortest possible time. If you have any custom product requirements, we are happy to re-engineer and manufacture products tailored to your market.

Production Facility

Durga Laboratory Glass Works has a state-of-the-art production facility spread over 100,000 sq. ft. The company is fully equipped to manufacture and assemble high volumes of products. Our production facility includes Metal Working Machines, Glass Blowing Machines, Woodworking Machines, and Plastic Injection Molding Machines. These automatic and semi-automatic machines ensure standardization and consistent quality of all our products.
